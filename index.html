Passionate software developer skilled in JavaScript, HTML, CSS and Swift. Over 20 years experience as a graphic artist, expert in Photoshop. I received my Front End Developer Certificate from FreeCodeCamp in October 2017, and am on track to finish the Full Stack Developer Certificate by early 2018.

In 2016, I co-founded Golden Platypus Apps, LLC along with dyslexia expert Marla Chandler. Our apps support the Orton-Gillingham approach for teaching those with dyslexia.

While dyslexics can learn spelling rules and practice them successfully for days in a row, they eventually forget. Research has shown that gamification stimulates executive level functioning, Golden Platypus apps harness this in an aim to increase retention and automaticity in spelling.

I did all the coding (front and back end; Swift and Core Data) and graphics for our apps. “Dunk the Duck,” “Catch the Finch” and “Dodge the Deluge” are in the app store now!
